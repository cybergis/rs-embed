Applications

One embedding, many tools. Each application turns the same vectors into something useful. The live demo here is retrieval — how alike are two places?
🧭 Region similarity
Right-click two places on the map (A, then B). Pick a model, then Compute the cosine similarity of their pooled embeddings.
🎨 Instant land-cover
Right-click one place on the map, pick a model and the number of clusters, then Segment. The grid embedding is k-means-clustered into land-cover-like regions and draped over the ROI as a transparent mask.
